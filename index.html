Who Was Kubra Rezai?

Kubra Rezai, also known as Shaghayegh, was a 26-year-old Afghan refugee living in Pishva, Varamin, Iran. She was a strong, hardworking woman simply trying to build a better life. Her life was tragically stolen in a horrific act of violence.

What Happened to Her?

On Tuesday, June 3, 2025, parts of Kubra’s mutilated body were discovered among piles of trash in Tehran. DNA tests confirmed her identity. She had gone missing 50 days earlier after leaving her home for work. She was abducted and brutally murdered.

Iranian authorities have failed to provide transparency or justice. On June 7, government forces prevented her funeral, refusing to issue an official burial permit — deepening the family's grief.

Why It Matters

Kubra's death is part of a pattern of violence and neglect faced by Afghan refugees — especially women — in Iran and beyond. This is a call for justice, accountability, and the protection of all refugee women.
